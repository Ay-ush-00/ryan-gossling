PERSONAL DETAILS




Gosling previously resided in New York City with his mixed-breed dog George.[15][187] He co-owns Tagine, a Moroccan restaurant in Beverly Hills, California.[188] He bought the restaurant on an impulse; he has said that he spent "all [his] money" on it,[189] spent a year doing the renovation work himself, and now oversees the restaurant's menus.[189][190]
Gosling dated his Murder by Numbers co-star Sandra Bullock from 2002 to 2003.[191] He then had a relationship with his The Notebook co-star Rachel McAdams from 2005 to 2007,[192] before they briefly reunited in 2008.[193][194]
Gosling has been in a relationship with his The Place Beyond the Pines co-star Eva Mendes since September 2011.[195] The couple have two daughters, one born in 2014 and the second in 2016.[196][197]
Gosling is supportive of various social causes. He has worked with PETA on a campaign to encourage KFC and McDonald's to use improved methods of chicken slaughter in their factories, and on a campaign encouraging dairy farmers to stop de-horning cows.[198][199][200] Gosling volunteered in Biloxi, Mississippi in 2005, as part of the clean-up effort following Hurricane Katrina.[201] He is a supporter of Invisible Children, Inc., a group that raises awareness about the LRA in Central Africa.[202] In 2005, Gosling traveled to Darfur refugee camps in Chad.[20][203] He was a speaker at Campus Progress' National Conference in 2008 where he discussed Darfur.[204] As part of his work with the Enough Project, he visited Uganda in 2007[205] and eastern Congo in 2010.[206]
